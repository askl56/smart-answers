# Do you need an adult or child passport?

[choice: child_or_adult]
* adult: Adult (aged 16 and over)
* child: Child (aged 15 or under)

* ips_application?(passport_country)
  * application_action in {applying renewing_old} => country_of_birth
  * ips_application_result_online?(passport_country) => ips_application_result_online
  * otherwise => ips_application_result
